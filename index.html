Welcome to TummyPuff Bakery!

TummyPuff Bakery Solutions is an international leader in the baking industry, producing one of the industry’s broadest ranges of products for customers in more than 100 countries. Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia,TummyPuff is dedicated to developing and providing solutions that drive customer growth and success. We offer a broad, innovative portfolio of premium quality bakery ingredients, finished products and services for retail and foodservice markets as well as artisan and industrial bakeries.Our goal is to ensure that our customers are better equipped to serve their customers. That’s why CSM connects ingredients and expertise, providing many of the industry’s most trusted brands, including Baker & Baker, Brill, MeisterMarken, Waldkorn, Ulmer Spatz and many others.
